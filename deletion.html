1 Clean Data Directly Inside the App

The absolute fastest and most thorough way to remove your data is to purge it directly from the local mobile sandbox directory. This deletes 100% of your habits, calendar schedules, streaks, and customization profiles from your hardware.

  • 1

    Open App Settings

    Navigate to the Profile tab in the bottom navigation bar and tap the Settings (Gear) icon.

  • 2

    Tap Reset Options

    Scroll down to the Data Integrity section and select Reset All App Data.

  • 3

    Confirm Action

    Confirm the validation pop-up. The local SQLite/Drift database will be instantly formatted, leaving absolutely zero records behind.

2 Revoking Cloud Backup Sync Access

If you activated the optional Google Drive synchronization, your backup is stored securely in your private cloud account. To dissolve this tie completely:

  • 1

    Disconnect via Google Security Center

    Go to your Google Third-Party Connections Portal.

  • 2

    Remove App Permissions

    Locate Smart Life Reminder in the list of connected platforms and choose Delete all connection details.

  • 3

    Clear Hidden Backup Data

    To wipe physical backups from Drive: Open Google Drive on desktop > Gear Icon > Settings > Manage Apps > Find Smart Life Reminder > Options > Delete Hidden App Data.
